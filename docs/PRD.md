# Park Vaahan - Product Requirements Document

## Project Overview

### Purpose
Park Vaahan is a web-based parking slot booking platform connecting vehicle owners with apartment/gated community parking spaces in Karnataka and Hyderabad, India, focusing on monthly rental arrangements.

### Target Users
- **Primary Users:** Vehicle owners seeking monthly parking spaces
- **Secondary Users:** Apartment/Gated community managers and resident welfare associations (RWAs)

### Business Goals
1. Simplify long-term parking space discovery in residential communities
2. Help apartment/gated communities monetize unused parking spaces
3. Provide secure, reliable monthly parking solutions
4. Generate leads for early market validation
